About

I am a computational scientist focused on translating complex, multi-dimensional biomedical data into actionable insights that accelerate therapeutic development and precision medicine.

Mission

Leverage rigorous computational methods, interpretable AI, and domain collaboration to improve decision quality in early discovery and translational research.

Focus Areas

  • Multi-omics integration for target & biomarker discovery
  • Medical imaging analytics (quantitative CT, segmentation, phenotyping)
  • Real-world data for patient stratification & disease trajectory modeling
  • Data platforms & interactive visualization tools for scientists

Approach & Philosophy

I value reproducibility, clarity, and collaboration. Robust methodology and transparent assumptions help teams make better, faster decisions.

Interests

Advancing explainable models in imaging + omics fusion, improving translational study design with computational endpoints, and enabling scientist self-service analytics.
